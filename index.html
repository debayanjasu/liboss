About Project

LibOs is a digital library management solution that simplifies how libraries operate. From cataloging books to managing members and tracking borrowings,sending issue and return mail to the students, LibOs brings efficiency and transparency to library management,it offers a clean interface, real-time updates, and secure access making it the perfect tool for schools, colleges, and community libraries.
